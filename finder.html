How it works

Your result unlocks after four choices.

Pick your goal, how you will use the stone, your budget comfort, and the visual direction you prefer. The recommendation only appears once all four are set — so the match is intentional, not random.

Honest about what stones are

Cultural tradition, not medical advice.

Stone associations on this site are drawn from documented historical and cultural use. The finder helps you find a stone that fits your intent and style — it does not make health, therapeutic, or outcome claims.
