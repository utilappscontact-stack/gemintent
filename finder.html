How the Stone Finder works

The Stone Finder matches your selected intent to semi-precious stones based on traditional and cultural associations documented across various civilisations, historical records, and traditional practices. Each stone in our database has been researched for its documented historical use across cultures — from ancient Greek and Roman traditions, to South Asian Ayurvedic practices, to indigenous traditions across multiple continents. This is informational and educational content, not a prescription or recommendation system.

This tool does not provide medical, therapeutic, or psychological advice. Stone associations described are based on cultural tradition and historical folklore, not scientific research. Please consult qualified healthcare professionals for any health concerns.

Some of the sellers we link to are affiliate partners. This means Gem Intent may earn a small commission if you purchase through our links, at no extra cost to you. Affiliate relationships do not influence which stones we recommend — our matching logic is based solely on intent and preference alignment.

Frequently asked questions

How does the Stone Finder work?

The tool matches your selected intent to semi-precious stones that have been traditionally associated with similar themes across various cultures and historical traditions. It is an informational guide, not a medical tool.

Are the recommendations based on science?

No. The associations are based on cultural history, traditional use, and documented folklore. Gem Intent does not make scientific or medical claims about stone properties or effects.

What if I do not believe in crystal properties?

That is completely fine. Many people use this tool purely for shopping guidance or to find a meaningful gift. The tool works equally well for skeptics and believers — the historical and cultural context is interesting regardless of personal belief.

Can I trust the recommended sellers?

We link to established, reputable sellers including Conscious Items, Etsy artisan sellers, and Amazon. We have an affiliate relationship with some of them, which means we may earn a small commission on sales. This never influences which stones we recommend.

Is the Stone Finder free?

Yes, completely free. No sign-up required, no data collected, no cost. The tool runs entirely in your browser and does not send any information to any server.
