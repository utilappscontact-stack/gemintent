Affiliate disclosure

How affiliate links work on this site.

Gem Intent may earn a commission when readers click through to a retailer or marketplace and make a purchase. The goal is to keep those placements visible, separated, and less intrusive than the core guidance.

What this means for readers.

If you buy through some links on this site, Gem Intent may receive a commission at no extra cost to you. That commission helps support the maintenance of the guides, finder, and stone pages.

What this does not mean.

A commission opportunity does not automatically make a stone better, a listing safer, or a product more authentic. The content should still tell readers what to check and where to be cautious.

How links are used.

Affiliate links can appear in dedicated product slots, shopping buttons, or retailer modules. The site keeps those placements visually separate so readers can recognise them easily.

Affiliate programmes we participate in.

Gem Intent currently participates in the following affiliate programmes: Amazon Associates, the Awin affiliate network (including Etsy), and the Conscious Items affiliate programme. When you click a product link and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you.

Plain summary

How affiliate links work on this site.

When you click a product link on a stone page and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission from the retailer. The price you pay is exactly the same whether you arrived via our link or found the product directly.
