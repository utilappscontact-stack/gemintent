Privacy policy

How visitor data is handled on Gem Intent.

This page explains what data third-party services on this site may collect, how it is used, and how you can manage your preferences.

What may be collected.

Like most content sites, Gem Intent may collect basic analytics information such as page visits, referral sources, device type, and approximate location. If a contact form or email is added, submitted information may also be retained so enquiries can be answered.

Affiliate and advertising links.

When you click a marketplace or retailer link, that destination may use its own tracking systems. Gem Intent may also use affiliate tracking to understand whether a referral resulted in a purchase. That process is standard for affiliate publishing.

Cookies and analytics.

If analytics or ad scripts are used, cookies or similar technologies may be involved. Update this page with the exact services you use before going live at full scale.

Your choices.

If you contact the site directly and want your message removed from records, you can request that through the published contact method.

In plain English

What this site does and does not collect.

Gem Intent does not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information. The Stone Finder runs entirely in your browser — your selections are not sent to any server.

No sign-up requiredThe finder and all stone guides are free and require no account or registration of any kind.
No first-party cookiesWe do not set cookies. Third-party services such as Google Analytics may set their own.
Affiliate clicksWhen you click a product link, the destination retailer may use cookies to track the referral. This is standard and disclosed.
